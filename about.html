History

Our company was founded in 1950 by a local Brookville native who loved the lake and all the history behind it. He noticed that it wasnâ€™t being fully utilized for his beauty so he created a small marina that would allow people on the lake to stop by and get supplies and snacks. As the years went on he expanded gradually into what we are today. We started selling boats in 1995 and starting taking trades in 2000. We also became official partners with our boat manufacturers in 2002 and have continued to benefit from that as well. We are now working with people from all over the country to meet there boating needs and expect to continue to grow within the next 5-10 years.

Ceo Profile

Welcome to Brookville Marina, our CEO is Jake Boyles. Jake has always been fascinated with the boating industry and being on the water. At the age of 15 he bought his first boat and its been an amazing ride ever since. Jake bought the marina 5 years ago and has worked to turn it around into a place where people all over the country come to buy and sell boats and to also experience the greatness that is Brookville Lake. Jake currently lives in Brookville and is very passionate about the boating community around town. You can contact Jake at Jake@brookvillemarina.com
